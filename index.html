NUCLEAR TREATIES & AGREEMENTS
IN FORCE
New START
Limits US + Russia to 1,550 deployed strategic warheads each and 700 delivery systems. Russia suspended participation Feb 2023. Expires Feb 2026 — no replacement negotiated.
IN FORCE
NPT (Non-Proliferation Treaty)
191 signatories. 5 recognized nuclear states (P5). India, Pakistan, Israel never signed. North Korea withdrew 2003. Foundation of non-proliferation regime since 1970.
NOT IN FORCE
CTBT (Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban)
Bans all nuclear explosions. 187 signed but NOT in force — USA, China, Iran, Egypt, Israel signed but NOT ratified. India, Pakistan, DPRK never signed. In limbo since 1996.
WITHDRAWN
INF Treaty
Banned ground-launched missiles 500-5,500 km range (US + Russia). US withdrew Aug 2019 citing Russian violations (9M729/SSC-8 missile). Enabled new missile deployments globally.
EXPIRED
ABM Treaty
Limited anti-ballistic missile systems ($0 US, Russia). US withdrew June 2002 (Bush) to build missile defense. Freed Russia to develop hypersonic weapons to defeat US defenses.
IN FORCE
TPNW (Ban Treaty)
Treaty on Prohibition of Nuclear Weapons. 93 ratifications. NO nuclear-armed state or NATO member has joined. Symbolic importance but no practical enforcement mechanism.
